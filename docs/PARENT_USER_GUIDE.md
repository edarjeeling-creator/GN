# Parent User Guide
## v1.0 Attendance Analytics Suite

Welcome to the Parent Portal for the Gyanoday Niketan Attendance Analytics Suite. This portal provides transparent, real-time access to your child's attendance records, alerts, and official certificates.

---

## 1. Login & Navigation
Log in using your Parent credentials. You will be greeted by the **Parent Dashboard**.

## 2. Key Features

### Multiple Ward Support
If you have multiple children enrolled at Gyanoday Niketan, you will see a toggle bar at the top of the screen. Simply click your child's name to switch the dashboard view instantly.

### Live Metrics
- **Today's Status**: See exactly if your child was marked Present, Absent, Late, or on Leave today.
- **Monthly Attendance %**: Tracks their overall attendance for the current month. Approved Medical Leaves do not negatively impact this score.
- **Consecutive Absences**: A live counter tracking how many days in a row your child has been absent.

### Official Certificate Generator
You can instantly generate and download a verified PDF attendance certificate.
- Features an automated QR code for validity tracking.
- Includes Principal's signature block.
- Perfect for external scholarship or medical documentation requirements.

## 3. Common Tasks

### Downloading a Certificate
1. Navigate to the **Attendance History** tab on your child's dashboard.
2. Click the **Official Certificate** button (with the download icon).
3. The PDF will generate and save directly to your device.

### Acknowledging an Alert
If your child misses multiple consecutive days, you will receive a System Alert.
1. Navigate to the **Alerts & Notifications** section.
2. Read the notification.
3. Click **Acknowledge Alert** to confirm to the school that you have seen the warning.

## 4. Troubleshooting & FAQs
### Common Errors & Solutions

**Q: "No Students Linked" error appears on my dashboard.**
> **Solution:** Your user account has not yet been mapped to your child's student profile in the database. Please contact the School Administration at admin@school.edu to have your accounts linked.

**Q: My child's attendance percentage seems lower than expected.**
> **Solution:** Remember that "Late" and "Half Day" statuses may impact the weighting depending on school policy. However, "Medical Leave" and excused "Leave" are excluded from the calculation.

**Q: I received an absence notification, but my child is at school!**
> **Solution:** If a teacher accidentally marks a student absent, they can correct it. Once corrected, the false notification will be invalidated in the system. Contact the Class Teacher via the contact panel on your dashboard to request a correction.
